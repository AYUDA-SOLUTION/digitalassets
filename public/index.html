About Affine
  • Affine Global Corporation and its operations are a new way of doing business. This Delaware company and its legal and personal relationships we refer to as “Affine”. The ability to scale quickly through open source collaboration is the reason for the Company’s accomplishments from a simple idea to a global ‘production ready’ exchange in less than two years.


  • Our mission is to safely and securely connect our institutional and retail customers to a global platform of multi-asset exchanges, clearing and settlement companies and central depositories integrated with robust risk management to trade in digital assets representing securities or non-financial tokens used in loyalty programs, eSports and autonomous driving. We call our global platform HDXe
